Smoking Health at a Glance

What are Electronic Cigarettes?

E-cigarettes (electronic nicotine delivery systems) emerged in the U.S. market in 2007 and have rapidly grown in popularity. E-cigarettes, or e-vaporizers, are devices that heat a liquid containing solvents, flavors, and often nicotine. Users inhale the resulting vapor.
